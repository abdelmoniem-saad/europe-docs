## Basecamp Routines

Surviving a semester means establishing protocols. 

**Groceries & Supply Lines**
- **Rema 1000 & Netto:** The backbone of the daily budget. Good for staples.
- **Føtex / Kvickly:** Upper tier. Better produce, higher prices.
- **Torvehallerne:** The glass-market luxury tier. Used strictly for treats or showing visitors around.

**Transit**
- The **Metro** runs 24/7. It is driverless and incredibly efficient.
- The **S-Tog** (red commuter trains) connects the wider suburbs.
- **Bikes:** The true Copenhagen way. The infrastructure is flawless, though the morning rush hour is intense.
