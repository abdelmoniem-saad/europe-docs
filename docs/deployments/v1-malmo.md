## Mission Brief

First half of a full Sweden day-trip, executed while fasting. Malmö was the morning sprint — flat, walkable, and freezing. The fasting clock was an advantage: zero time wasted on lunch breaks meant pure, uninterrupted exploration from arrival until the 14:00 train to Lund.

---

## System Logs

### Phase 1 — Waterfront & The Giant `08:30 – 10:00`

- Arrived via Flixbus. Skipped the city center on arrival and walked straight towards the water.
- Reached **Västra Hamnen** (The Western Harbour) district.
- Walked up to the **Turning Torso** — Scandinavia's tallest building, 190 m of twisted steel and glass.
- Continued along the **Ribersborg beach** waterfront promenade.
- Clear morning view of the **Öresund Bridge** connecting Sweden to Denmark.

!!! note "Runtime Condition"
    Morning air was freezing but incredibly crisp. The cold + fasting combo burns serious energy — thermals and double socks were essential.

### Phase 2 — The Fortress & The Parks `10:15 – 12:00`

- Walked from the waterfront to **Malmöhus Castle** — Scandinavia's oldest surviving Renaissance fortress, surrounded by a deep moat.
- Circled the castle through **Kungsparken** (The King's Park) and **Slottsparken**.
- Found the hidden **Slottsmöllan** — a historic windmill tucked inside the park.
- Quick warm-up stop inside the **Malmö City Library** (Stadsbiblioteket). The **"Calendar of Light"** wing — a massive, multi-story glass wall facing the park — is stunning architecture and a welcome 15-minute refuge from the cold.

### Phase 3 — Old Town (Gamla Staden) `12:15 – 13:45`

- Entered the historic core and walked into **Stortorget**, the massive main square.
- Saw the spectacular **Rådhuset** (City Hall) with its ornate Renaissance facade.
- Crossed into **Lilla Torg** (The Little Square) — the postcard-perfect, cobblestoned square surrounded by half-timbered houses dating back to the 1500s.
- Walked down **Södergatan**, the main pedestrian shopping street, taking in the bustle.

### Phase 4 — Transfer `14:00`

- Walked to **Malmö Central Station**.
- Boarded train to Lund C via **Skånetrafiken** (~60 SEK / ~€5.50).
- Travel time: **15 minutes exactly**. Rested legs on the ride.
