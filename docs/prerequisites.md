# Prerequisites

Everything that needs to be sorted before the first trip.

---

## Essentials

| Item | Purpose | Notes |
|:-----|:--------|:------|
| **ESN Card** | Discount card for exchange students | Unlocks 20 kg free Ryanair baggage, museum discounts, and FlixBus deals |
| **Zabihah App** | Find Halal restaurants worldwide | Primary tool for locating verified Halal food in every city |
| **Tuna Cans** | Emergency meals | Budget-saving fallback for expensive cities (Scandinavia especially) |
| **Backpack (40L)** | Carry-on only travel | Keeps things light — no checked bags, no waiting at carousels |

## Apps & Accounts

- **Rejseplanen** — Denmark's public transit planner. Essential for getting around Copenhagen.
- **Ryanair** — Cheap flights across Europe. Book 4–6 weeks early.
- **FlixBus** — Budget intercity buses when trains are too expensive.
- **Google Maps (Offline)** — Download city maps before each trip.
- **Wise / Revolut** — Multi-currency card, no foreign transaction fees.
- **Hostelworld** — Booking hostels. Sort by rating, not price.

## Packing Philosophy

!!! tip "One bag, every trip"
    If it doesn't fit in the 40L backpack, it doesn't come. This keeps flights cheap (carry-on only) and movement fast. Laundry every 3 days.
